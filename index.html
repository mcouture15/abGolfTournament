Andy Blackwell Memorial Golf Tournament 2015

Sunday August 16th 12:00pm-10:00pm

Hawk Ridge Golf & Country Club, Orillia ON

- About Us -

This year's Andy Blackwell Memorial Golf Tournament is taking place on August 16, 2015 at Hawk Ridge Golf & Country Club in Orillia ON. This annual event, established in 2014, was created in memory of a young man whose passion and enthusiasm never ceased to amaze those around him. Its purpose is twofold: first to bring together friends, family, and members of the community, and second to raise funds contributing to an annual scholarship in his name. Those who had the opportunity to know or know of Andy understand and remember his innate ability to bring people together. We intend, through this annual event, to accomplish exactly that on a yearly basis.

"If you can't be good, be good at it" - so let's make sure we do this right once again this summer. Thank you for your support and we look forward to seeing you on August 16th!
